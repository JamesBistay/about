Network Administrator

Alabang
Full-time

Job Description

We are looking for a skilled Network Administrator to manage and maintain our educational institution's network infrastructure. You will ensure the stability, security, and performance of our IT systems while supporting staff and students.

Responsibilities

  • Install, configure, and maintain network equipment including routers, switches, and firewalls
  • Monitor network performance and troubleshoot network issues
  • Implement and maintain network security measures

  • Strong knowledge of networking protocols and concepts
  • Experience with network security and monitoring tools
