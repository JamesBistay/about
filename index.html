Our Story

San Beda College Alabang was established to provide quality education rooted in Benedictine values. Over the years, it has grown into a prestigious institution known for academic excellence.

Our IT Services Department has been at the forefront of digital transformation within the educational sector, implementing innovative solutions that enhance learning experiences and administrative processes.

With a dedicated team of professionals, we continue to evolve and adapt to the changing technological landscape, ensuring that our community benefits from cutting-edge digital services.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To develop individuals committed to faith, knowledge, and service through innovative digital solutions that enhance the educational experience.

We strive to create technological environments that foster learning, collaboration, and efficiency across all aspects of academic and administrative life.

Vision: To be a leading educational institution recognized for holistic formation, academic excellence, and technological innovation that prepares students for success in an increasingly digital world.

Our Values

At San Beda College Alabang, our core values guide everything we do, from educational programs to technological initiatives:

Faith

We embrace and promote Catholic Benedictine values, integrating them with technological advancement to serve our community better.

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in education and digital services, continuously improving our offerings to meet evolving needs.

Service

We are committed to serving our community through innovative digital solutions that enhance learning and administrative processes.
